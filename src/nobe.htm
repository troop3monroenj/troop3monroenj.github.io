Camp No-Be-Bo-Sco

Directions to Camp No-Be-Bo-Sco

  • Take Route 18 North to Interstate 287 North.
  • Exit onto Route 206 North at Exit 22B (exit is past I-78).
  • Route 206 joins up with Interstate 80 West.
  • Get on I-80 West.
  • Take Interstate Route 80 to Exit 12 in New Jersey .
  • Take Exit 12 North (Hope/Blairstown, Warren County Route 521).
  • Follow Route 521 to Blairstown (4 miles) and its junction with Route 94.
  • Turn left onto Route 94 and follow to the first light. Turn right at the light, then go straight, following Warren County Route 602 up to the top of the hill, and turn right at the stop sign on the aptly named ‘hairpin turn’.
  • Take the first road on the left. This is Millbrook Road and is marked by a white directional sign which lists No-Be-Bo-Sco.
  • Follow Millbrook Road for approximately 4 miles. Turn right onto Birch Ridge Road, indicated by name on a yellow road sign and marked by a directional sign for YMCA Camp Mason.
  • Follow Birch Ridge Road past YMCA Camp Mason on the left. Turn left onto the first road on the left. This is Shannon Road.
  • Follow Shannon Road through the ‘S’ curve to a lake on the right. The road narrows as it passes the lake and then widens. Where the road widens is exactly where Sand Pond Road (with its stone curbing) intersects Shannon Road at about a 45-degree reverse angle. Turn a SHARP left onto Sand Pond Road.
  • Follow Sand Pond Road for one-half mile to the camp entrance on the right, marked by the camp sign. Proceed down the dirt road to the parking area, where signs will direct you to check-in.
