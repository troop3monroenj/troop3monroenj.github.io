Pouch Camp

Directions to Pouch Camp

  • Take NJ Turnpike north to Exit 13.
  • Take Goethals Bridge into Staten Island.
  • Stay on SI Expressway. Exit at "Bradley Avenue" exit.
  • Get into right lane, make a right onto Bradley Avenue.
  • Follow Bradley to end, make a left onto Brielle.
  • Go past the school (on right), make a right onto Manor Road.
  • Follow Manor Road approx. 1.5 miles to camp entrance on left (at flashing yellow light).
