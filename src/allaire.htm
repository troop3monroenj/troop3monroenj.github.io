Allaire State Park

Directions to Allaire

  • Take Monmouth Rd to Texas Rd (Rte 520), make left.
  • Stay on Rte 520 East to Rte 18 South.
  • Exit Rte 18 onto Rte 34 South.
  • Stay on Rte 34 South through traffic circle.
  • Just past exit for Interstate 195, exit right for Allaire (you'll see sign for Allaire State Park).
    ** If you go under Garden State Parkway, you went too far.
  • At the STOP sign, make a left onto Rte 524.
    ** Ignore the sign that says Allaire State Park is a right turn - we're going to a different area of the park.
  • Make a right onto Hospital Rd.
  • At STOP sign, make a left.
  • Entrance to the group campsites is 1/4 mile down on your left (immediately after intersection for Allenwood Lakewood Rd).
    ** If you go under overpass, you went too far.

To get back home from Allaire

  • Make right after leaving campsite entrance.
  • Make a right onto Hospital Rd.
  • Make a right on Atlantic (Rte 524).
  • Follows signs for Garden State Parkway.
  • At traffic light at intersection of Rte 34, make a left.
