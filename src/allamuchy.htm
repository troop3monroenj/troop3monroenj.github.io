Allamuchy

Directions to Allamuchy

  • Take Route 18 North to Interstate 287 North.
  • Exit onto Route 206 North at Exit 22B (exit is past I-78).
  • Route 206 joins up with Interstate 80 West for about 1 mile.
  • Exit I-80 West at Exit 25 (Route 206 North)
  • Stay on Route 206 North until you reach Waterloo Road (only a mile or so).
  • Make a left onto Waterloo Road. Proceed 2 1/2 miles.
  • Soon after passing the Waterloo Village entrance, the Camp entrance will be on your right.
