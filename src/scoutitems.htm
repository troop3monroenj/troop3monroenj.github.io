Troop 3 Resources

Items you will need for Boy Scouts:

  • Boy Scout shirt
  • Number 3 patch for the sleeve
  • Monmouth Council patch for the sleeve
  • Boy Scout epaulets (for shoulders of shirt)
  • Boy Scout belt
  • Boy Scout pants
  • Boy Scout neckerchief & slide
  • Backpack for camping
  • Sleeping bag (a warm one – we camp in the winter too!)
  • Canteen
  • Mess kit & utensil set
  • Compass
  • Boy Scout Handbook
  • Rain poncho
  • Individual 1st aid kit
