Troop Committee Notes

Updates from 3/9/2020

  • Full year Scout Dues will be collected in the Fall in order to pay Charter fees
    • During Fall collection period, Scouts aging out before the end of calendar year will pay $30 dues in order to be considered "current" (i.e. be eligible for Awards, Scolarships, etc.)
    • Scout transfers (including Webelos) in the Spring/Summer will pay $30 transfer fee, then regular dues in the Fall
     
  • Scout records will be retained by the Troop for 5 years after the Scout's 18th birthday
