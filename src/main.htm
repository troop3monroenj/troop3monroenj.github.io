About Us

Our troop is located in Monroe Township, NJ. We have been chartered since 1978. Troop 3 is proud to have as our chartered organization the Jewish War Veterans Post 609 / Post 395.

Troop meetings are held weekly, and we try to run a camping trip, hike, or other activity every month. We also participate in community service projects.

We are proud to uphold and follow the principles of the Boy Scouts of America. Since 1910, millions of boys have joined Scouting because they liked what the Boy Scouts of America had to offer.

Troop 3 has produced over 80 Eagle Scouts and the list continues to grow.
